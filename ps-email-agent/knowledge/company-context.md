# Pallet Solutions USA - Company Context

This file provides business context for the AI agent. Edit this to teach the agent about your business.

## Company Overview

Pallet Solutions USA supplies and delivers pallets to businesses across the region.

## Products

### Standard Pallets
- **Grade A 48x40** - Premium quality, minimal repairs
- **Grade B 48x40** - Good quality, may have cosmetic repairs
- **Grade C 48x40** - Economy grade, functional but visible wear

### Specialty Pallets
- Custom sizes available on request
- Heat-treated (HT) pallets for export
- Plastic pallets for specific applications

## Common Customer Phrases and What They Mean

| Customer Says | What They Mean |
|--------------|----------------|
| "the usual" | Same product/quantity as their last order |
| "same as last time" | Repeat their most recent order |
| "regular order" | Their typical recurring order |
| "ASAP" | Within 24-48 hours if possible |
| "next week" | Monday-Friday of the following week |
| "end of month" | Last business day of current month |
| "a load" | Typically a full truckload (~1000 pallets) |
| "a few" | Usually 50-100 pallets |
| "GMA pallets" | Standard 48x40 grocery pallets |

## Delivery Information

- Standard lead time: 2-3 business days
- Rush delivery available (24 hours) for additional fee
- Delivery hours: 6 AM - 6 PM unless specified
- Forklift required at delivery location
- Driver will call 30 minutes before arrival

## Pricing Guidelines

- Pricing varies by customer agreement
- Volume discounts available for 500+ pallets
- Contract customers have locked-in pricing
- New customers: provide quotes, don't commit to pricing

## Vendor Network

Orders are fulfilled through regional vendor partners based on:
- Customer delivery location
- Product availability
- Existing vendor relationships

## Important Policies

1. **Credit terms**: Net 30 for approved customers
2. **Minimum order**: 50 pallets (exceptions for repeat customers)
3. **Cancellation**: 24 hours notice required
4. **Damaged pallets**: Report within 48 hours of delivery
5. **Returns**: Arranged on case-by-case basis

## Red Flags (Flag for Human Review)

- First-time customers requesting large orders
- Requests for credit without established account
- Complaints about quality or service
- Invoice disputes
- Unusual delivery locations
- Requests that seem outside normal business

## Contact Escalation

- Urgent issues: Flag as HIGH priority
- Invoice/billing: Always flag for accounting review
- Complaints: Always flag for operations manager
- New large accounts: Flag for sales team
